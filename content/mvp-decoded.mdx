This episode of 'Brick by Brick' dives into MVPs (Minimum Viable Products) in the SaaS world. We talk about the great stuff, the problems, and the big oopsies of creating MVPs. Hear stories of success and mistakes from a SaaS builder and seller. Learn what works, what doesn't, and how to make your MVP shine. Perfect for anyone interested in SaaS, whether you're starting out or just curious. Tune in to get the scoop on MVPs!
